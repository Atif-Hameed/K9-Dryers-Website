What makes a high-quality dog dryer?

3 things that make a dog dryer the best

1. Adjustable high velocity air speed

Air speed, not heat, is actually the most important factor of a dog dryer. A higher speed means more air flow, which means your dog's coat will become drier in less time.

The increased noise, however, could alarm your dog. That's why our dog dryers have an adjustable air speed - depending on how well your dog takes to getting their coat dried, you can have the coat dried in more or less time.

2. The temperature

With dog dryers, the coat is dried mostly through the power of the air. However, it's still important that the temperature of the air is not too cold or hot to be uncomfortable for your furry friend. That's why our dog dryers give off the perfect warm temperature, so your dog never becomes overheated and sits comfortably while they're being groomed.

3. How it's made

When it comes to dog hair dryers, metal construction is the way to go. Cheap knockoffs from overseas are made with flimsy plastic parts that melt and stop working under any regular amount of use.

In addition to the overall construction, be sure your dryer can swap in different accessories easily. For K-9 Dryers, that means flat tip nozzles for most coats, round tip nozzles that are designed for blowing out undercoats, a high-quality hose that won't kink or melt during extended use, and a resuable filter that's easy to clean, and saves the earth - and you some money - over the long term.
